📝 Share Your Reading Experience

As readers ourselves, we're fascinated by how people choose between e-books and print books. Your personal experiences and preferences are invaluable to understanding the real-world dynamics of this ongoing evolution in reading culture. Whether you're a devoted print book lover, an enthusiastic e-reader user, or someone who enjoys both formats, we'd love to hear from you!

"Every reader's journey is unique. Your story helps us understand the diverse ways people connect with books in the digital age."

Tell Us About Your Reading Habits

✅ Thank you for sharing your reading experience! Your insights help us understand how readers navigate the evolving world of books.

Why Your Reading Habits Matter

Understanding real reader experiences helps us:

  • Validate Research: Compare academic findings with actual reader behavior
  • Identify Trends: Spot emerging patterns in format preferences
  • Understand Context: Learn when and why people choose different formats
  • Bridge Generations: Discover how age affects reading format choices
  • Improve Analysis: Refine our understanding with diverse perspectives

What Readers Are Saying

"I love my Kindle for travel, but nothing beats the feel of a real book on lazy Sunday afternoons. Why choose when you can have both?" - Niaz Makhdum, Teacher

"As a student, e-books save me money and space. But for my favorite novels, I still buy print editions to keep on my shelf forever." - Sadia, University Student

"I thought I'd never switch to e-books, but the adjustable font size changed everything. My aging eyes are grateful!" - Dr. Nikhil, Librarian
